Welcome to Our Pet Adoption Center!

Our mission is to find loving homes for pets in need. We have a variety of pets including dogs, cats, rabbits, and more who are waiting to meet you! Adopting a pet is a wonderful way to bring joy to your life and give a furry friend a forever home.

Browse through our available pets, learn more about the adoption process, and see how you can get involved. Whether you're looking to adopt, volunteer, or donate, there's a place for you here at our center.

Our Mission

Is to prevent the crowding of shelters by relying on the communities for shelters.

The shelters in Arizona are operating at critical capacity and to accommodate more pets are dividing the living spaces of current animals to allow for more pets to be rescued.

Adoption Process

Step 1

Find the pet you want on the pet's tab and notate the pet type and age. These are all that are used to determine the price.

Step 2

Use the form below to calculate the adoption fee. This fee will be the total price of the adoption.

Pet Adoption Fee Calculator

Step 3

Go to the contacts page

In the contacts page you can email, text, call, or fill out the message form. Let us know that you wish to adopt the pet. Make sure to include the pet name and age.

About Us

We are Pet Adoption Center

We are a nonprofit group that seeks to solve one of Arizona's problems: overcrowding in shelters. Many people don't realize that when a pet is sent to a shelter, the shelter often needs to be divided to allow for more room for pets. To solve this issue, we are tackling this by relying on the community to provide homes for these pets.

We will make the difference that these pets need. Whether that be providing a home or a donation to the pet. Here at Pet adoptions we will make sure these pets live a fullfiling life and to do this requires the community.
